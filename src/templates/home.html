How It Works

1

Configure Your Scan

Enter your website URL and choose your WCAG version and compliance level. Set crawl depth, page limits, and concurrency based on your needs.

2

Automated Scanning

CATS crawls your site, discovering pages through sitemaps or following links. Each page is tested using axe-core for comprehensive accessibility analysis.

3

Review Results

Access detailed reports showing violations, warnings, and passes. Each issue includes specific WCAG references and remediation guidance.

4

Fix and Re-test

Use the actionable insights to fix accessibility issues. Run new scans to verify fixes and track improvement over time.
